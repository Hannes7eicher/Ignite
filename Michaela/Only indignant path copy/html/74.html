She called her mom and as soon she heard her voice her tears started to push forward again. It was a lot of emotions and it was supposed to be a great day today, after all. Not something like this.

"I did not get the promotion mom," She said.

"Oh no, I'm so sorry, I thought it was already decided. Do you know why it got changed?" Her mom said.

"Ehm, no, not exactly...". Sophie did not know how to tell her mom that Alex had something to do with it. She could barely believe it herself.

"Maybe you should try to do something about it? I mean I would be angry, it is not fair to make such a promise and then break it."

"Yes, maybe it can still be fixed.."

"I think so. But this is truly unfair. Don't give up, darling, I believe you will find a solution."

Sophie decided to go home after all.
